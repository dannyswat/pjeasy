# pjeasy

## Objectives
- To support an IT team to manage projects easily
- To allow individual team member to schedule and log daily tasks
- To ease the IT team supports to users and stack holders

## Features
- To manage projects with ideas, designs, features, issues, service tickets
- Convert the project items to assignees' tasks and allow team members prioritizing own tasks
- Allow team members to update task status and the project items
- To support team members to present the project milestones, sprint status and performance metrics
